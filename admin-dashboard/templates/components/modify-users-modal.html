Toggle Access

If a user no longer needs access to MarinTrace, you can disable their account (preserving their report history and interactions), but blocking them further access. You can also enable their account at any time.

Resend Invitation

If a user does not redeem their invitation in 48 hours, their link will expire. You can re-send invites to those users here.

The button below will remove a user's access to MarinTrace and delete their report history and contacts. Please note that this is not reversible, and the deleted information cannot be recovered.

Since you have access to multiple campuses, you can use the "Delete Campus History" button to clear the user's report history for the current campus (but keeping it everywhere else).
